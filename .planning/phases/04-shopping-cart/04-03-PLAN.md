7. Confirm the modal shows a stepper (not "Add to cart") since the card is already in cart

9. Close the modal. Find a DIFFERENT card tile and click it to open the modal
10. Click "Add to cart" in the modal -- badge should increment

12. Find a card with small quantity (e.g., qty 1 or 2). Add it to cart

14. On the cart page (step 17+), try typing a number exceeding stock in the input -- should cap and show "Only X available"

**Cart persistence:**
15. With items in cart, do a full page refresh (Cmd+R / F5)

23. Confirm the sticky bottom bar shows correct format: "X cards -- $Y.ZZ"

28. Confirm empty state: "Your cart is empty" message with "Browse cards" button
29. Click "Browse cards" -- should navigate back to home page (/)

**Persistence on cart page:**
30. Add some items, navigate to /cart, refresh the page
